Eligibility

  • You have to be a US Citizen.
  • You have to be a resident of the state you're voting in.
  • You cannot be currently serving a prison term, on parole, or in another form of post-release supervision if you are a resident in any of the following states:
    • Alaska
    • Arkansas
    • Georgia
    • Idaho
    • Iowa
    • Kansas
    • Louisiana
    • Maryland
    • Minnesota
    • Missouri
    • Nebraska
    • Nevada
    • New Jersey
    • New Mexico
    • North Carolina
    • Oklahoma
    • South Carolina
    • Texas
    • Washington
    • West Virginia
    • Wisconsin
  • You must be atleast 18 years of age.

Registration

  • Find out your state's deadline for registration.
    • Click this link to find out your states deadline
  • Choose one of the four ways to register to vote:
    • You can register online in certain states
    • Send in a National Mail Voter Registration Form
    • Register in person
    • Fill out an absentee ballot
  • If you're already registered to vote, you will need to register again if you've changed your name, address, or if you'd like to officially change your political party affiliation.
